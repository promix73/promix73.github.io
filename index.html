About Us

Kingshouse Learning Centre is a dynamic and innovative Information and Communication Technology (ICT) firm, dedicated to providing cutting-edge ICT solutions and services to individuals, NGOs & Corporate organizations. We are a globally certified professionals ICT firm by CISCO international to provide basic and core foundational training in ICT for all individuals at all levels.

PROFESSIONAL CERTIFIED IT COURSES

We help in developing the individual along the ICT lines. This is more so as ICT know-how has become a necessity in profitably navigating through today's world.

100% HANDS-ON AND PRATICAL TRAINING

We are dedicated to providing our students with 100% hands-on practical on all courses, where they can see and test realistic applications of is being taught.

CREATING E-LEARNING FRAMEWORK

Welcome to our e-learning framework, designed to provide an optimal learning experience for students in the digital age. Our framework combines the best practices.

PRACTICAL SUPPORT AND CONTENT CREATIONAL TRAINING

Proper management, maintenance, and repair of ICT systems. Our responsibilities include diagnosing and resolving network issues, installing and configuring.
